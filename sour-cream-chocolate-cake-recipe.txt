Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 2 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1 cup water
 - 3/4 cup sour cream
 - 1/4 cup shortening
 - 1 1/4 teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla
 - 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
 - 2 eggs
 - 4 ounces melted unsweetened chocolate (cool)
 - Sour Cream Chocolate Frosting

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch or three 8-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. Measure all ingredients except frosting into large mixer bowl. Mix 1/2 minute on low speed, scraping bowl constantly. Beat 3 minutes high speed, scraping bowl occasionally. 

 3. Pour into pan(s). 
 
 4. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean, or until top springs back when touched lightly with finger. Cool completely.

 5. Frost with Sour Cream Chocolate Frosting.
